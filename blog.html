Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2024

The Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IITJ) offers a wide array of online and certification programs to promote lifelong learning and skill enhancement. These courses are tailored to meet the evolving demands of various industries, enabling professionals and students to stay at the forefront of their fields. Our mission at IIT Jodhpur is to build a community of forward-thinking individuals who are committed to leveraging their skills for positive impact. Through our flexible online courses, participants from across the globe can now access high-quality education and be part of our vibrant academic network.


In addition to technical fields, IITJ also offers interdisciplinary programs, covering areas like Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, and IoT. Each course is carefully crafted by industry experts and experienced faculty, ensuring that learners are well-prepared to meet the challenges of a fast-paced, technology-driven world.
